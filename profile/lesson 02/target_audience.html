Description of Target Audience

The target audience for this project is the general public who lives in West Texas. This will help you if you are a working in oil and gas. If you are trucking supplies from a long distance, or just passing through!

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1:

    • Two College engineering students, Canyon and his girlfriend Emily, are planning a trip to the family ranch. They are planning to go on a cattle drive later the next day . They are checking the weather to see if they can be outside with the cattle , or if they will need to put the cattle in away because of the high wind and possible tornadoes.

  • Scenario 2:

    • John a single guy has a hot date tonight, checks the daily weather forcase before picking up his newly dryleaned suit. He needs to know if rain is expected and what the high and low temperatures are forcasted to be.

  • Scenario 3:

    • Mr. Johnson is planning a surprise birthday party for his wife Lexi. To make sure that the party is as great as possible, he need to know the weather forcasted for the next few days. If it is going to rain, he should plan to bring an umbrella. If it is going to be windy, they should plan to get dirty since wind brings the dirt here, and if it is going to be a storm, he should plan to cancel.

  • Scenario 4:

    • The local community soccer association is hosting a soccer tournament for the local junior teams ages 5-12. They need to know the expected temperatures and weather forcasted for the weekend, so they can send out an email to all the parents. They will keep track of the forcasted weather in case they need to reschedule the tournament.

  • Scenario 5:

    • The Piano Guys are planning to visit! They need to know the weather forcasted for the next few days, so they can plan accordingly and play amazing music for the Texans!
