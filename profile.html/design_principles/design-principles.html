Visual Hierarchy

Spotify

The way that spotify uses visual hierarchy is by showing the importance through bigger and more colorful items in contrast to smaller ones.A good example of this is the title and the button

Hicks Law

amazon

Hicks law states that the more optios we have the more difficult it is to reach a decision. Here amazon has a variety of sellers of many different items where the user can be swayed depending on price, review, shipping cost, shipping time etc

Fitts Law

Home Goods

The website uses whitespace by focusing in the main items like the name of the game and the play button. And leaving the other space without text to direct the viewer's eye.
