Purpose

The purpose of this site is two fold:
  • Provide a weather forecast for the next five days for several West Texas cities. The forecast is provided by [National Weather Service, Open Weather Map, or other website] and is updated every hour.
  • Provide a site that includes the idea of event promotion, relevant news, and other creative content that is relevant.
